Shrimp Scampi

Decription

Easy-to-make shrimp scampi that will leave you mouth-watering with this delicious dinner!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 pound uncooked shrimp (31-40 per pound), peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oreganp
  • 1/2 cup grates Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
  • Hot cooked angel hair pasta

Steps

  1. In a 10-in. ovenproof skilletm saute garlic in butter and oil until fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp, lemon juice, pepper, and oregano; cook and stir until shrimp turn pink.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese, bread crumbs, and parsley.
  4. Broil 6in. from the heat for 2-3 minutes or until topping is golden brown.
  5. Serve with pasta.
