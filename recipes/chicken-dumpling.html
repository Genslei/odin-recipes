Chicken and Dumplings

Decription

Best chicken and dumplings recipe to keep the whole family happy with each bite!

Ingredients

  • 3 celery ribs, chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, sliced
  • 3 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 3 cups cubed cooked chicken breast
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1-2/3 cups reduced-fat biscuit/baking mix
  • 2/3 cup fat-free milk

Steps

  1. In a Dutch overn coated with cooking spray, cook and stir celery and carrots over medium heat until tender for about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in broth, chicken, and seasonings.
  3. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to a gentle simmer.
  4. For dumplings, mix biscuit mix and milk until a soft dough forms.
  5. Drop by tablespoonfuls on top of the simmering liquid.
  6. Reduce heat to low; cover and cook until a toothpick inserted in dumplings comes out clean (do not lift cover during the first 10 minutes), 10-15 minutes.
